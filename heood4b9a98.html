Presentation of Visual Stimuli

Stimuli were generated with an LCD projector (Samsung, SP F10M) at a refresh rate of 60Hz, controlled with custom software written in Octave based on Psychtoobox.
The light path contained two motorized filter wheels. One wheel contained a set of natural density (ND) filters (Thorlabs NE10B-A to NE50B-A), with optical densities from 1 (ND1) to 5 (ND5), that were used to control the stimulus intensity in logarithmic steps. Another wheel contained a set of filters, that were used to achieve defined transmission properties. 
Recordings were performed with filters decreasing the stimulus intensity by 1-2 log units. For two-photon targeted patch-clamp, we use 750nm filter 
The projector produced a light spectrum that ranged from ~430nm to ~670nm. The power produced by the projector was 240 mW/cm2 at the retina. The output of the projector ranges from black (0) to white (255) pixels. All stimuli were gray-scale images with pixel values between 0 (black) and 255 (white). The background was kept at 30 (gray).
