Visual response properties of the superior colliculus
Dräger and Hubel were the first to report the distinct visual response properties of individual neurons in the superfical layers of the mouse SC (Dräger and Hubel, 1975a,b). They found SC neurons respond best to a small, slowly moving object of any shape within the receptive-field and a subset of SC neruons are direction-slective to the moving stimuli. In later research, Zhang et al conducted a quantitative and thorough analysis of visual response properties of were conducted in anesthetized mice (Zhang et al, 2010). They revealed the ON/OFF responses to flashing spot, and detailed how individual SC neruons are tuned to direction selectivity,
