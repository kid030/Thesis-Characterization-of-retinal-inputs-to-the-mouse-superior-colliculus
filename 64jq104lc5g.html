Preparation of Retinas

For in-vitro recordings of RGCs, we sued mice that had been injected with HSV into the Pbg or LP and RV into the SC to label circuit-specific retinal ganglion cells. After 5-10 days of rabies injection, retinas were isolated from mice that were dark-adapted for a minimum of 30 minutes. Retina isolation was done under deep red illumination in Ringer's medium (110mM NaCl, 2.5mM KCL, 1mM CaCl2, 1.6mM MgCl2, 10mM D-glucose, 22mM NaHCO3, bubbled with 5% CO2 / 95% O2, PH 7.4 ).
