Lamination and topographic alignment of inputs to the superior colliculus. (A) Contralateral RGCs along the temporal-nasal (T-N) axis of the retina project along the anterior-posterior (A-P) of the SC. The dorsal-ventral (D-V) axis of the retina projects along the lateral-medial (L-M) axis of the SC. (B) A schematic dagtram showing the laminated structure of the SC and inputs from the retina, the visual cortex (V1), and the somatosensory cortex (S1). Each projection is mapped topographical and aligned with the others. Abbreviations: SC, superior colliculus; uSGC, upper stratum griseum superficial; ISGS, lower stratum griseum superficial; SO, stratum opticum; SGI, stratum griseum intermediale
